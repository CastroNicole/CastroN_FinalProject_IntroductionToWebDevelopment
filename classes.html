Yoga Classes

Swedish Massage
Swedish massage is a gentle full-body massage. It can help release muscle knots, and it’s also a good choice for when you want to fully relax during a massage.
Hot Stone Massage
Hot stone massage is best for people who have muscle pain and tension or who simply want to relax.
Aromatheraphy Massage
Aromatherapy massages are best for people who enjoy scent and want to have an emotional healing component to their massage.
Deep Tissue Massage
Deep tissue massage uses more pressure than a Swedish massage. It’s a good option if you have muscle problems.
Sports Massage
Sports massage is a good option if you have a repetitive use injury to a muscle, such as what you may get from playing a sport.
Trigger Point Massage
Trigger point massage is best suited for people who have injuries, chronic pain, or a specific issue or condition.
Shiatsu Massage
Shiatsu massage is best for people who want to feel relaxed and relieve stress, pain, and tension. It’s a Japanese type of massage.
Thai Massage
Thai massage is best for people who want a more active form of massage and want to reduce and relieve pain and stress.
